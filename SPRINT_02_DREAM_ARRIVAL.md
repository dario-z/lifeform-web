# SPRINT 02 — Dream Arrival

## Goal

Make Dreams feel like an event, not just a hidden archive.

## Implemented

- NEW DREAM pill in the menu
- Dreaming… status in the menu
- dormant sprite while Dream generation is active
- unread Dream highlight in the Dreams panel
- Ask about this dream
- Copy dream
- less melodramatic Dream interpretation rules

## No database changes

This sprint reuses the existing `dreams` table from Sprint 01.

## Design note

The Dream itself remains a symbolic fragment. The interpretation should not always be dramatic or tragic. The Lifeform may interpret a Dream as strange, funny, absurd, mundane, playful, unresolved, or emotionally light when appropriate.
